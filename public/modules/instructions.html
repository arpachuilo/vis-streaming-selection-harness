Instructions

Hello, for the next while you will be completing some trials.

What are these trials?

Once you beign you will see dots moving across the display. Your goal is to accurately click as many red dots as possible, once a red dot is clicked a new one will pop up somewhere on the screen. You must click as many dots as you can within 5-10 seconds. After time is up you will be prompted with a screen where you will click the number representing how many blue dots you saw on the screen right before time was up.
