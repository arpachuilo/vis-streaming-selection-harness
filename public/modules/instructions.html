Instructions

Hello, for the next while you will be completing some trials.

What are these trials?

Your goal is to click as many red dots as possible, once a red dot is clicked a new one will pop up somewhere on the screen. You must click as many dots as you can within 5-10 seconds. After time is up you will be prompted with a screen where you will click the number representing how many blue dots you saw on the screen right before time was up.

How do I tell what's what?

Your cursor is a crosshair that moves with your mouse, whenever your crosshair is over a dot it's border will darken and get thicker. Whenever you click on a dot it will flash. After clicking a red dot the new red dot will enter flashing.

How do these techniques work?

Each technique has a different freeze region which is shown via a gray, highlighted area on your screen. When you hit 'Shift' the dots inside the highlighted region will freeze in place.

NOTE: Once a red dot is frozen and it's original goes off screen you will only have 2 seconds to click it.

Will I be able to test the techniques before using them?

Of course! Before using any new technique/trail combination you will be allowed practice. You may practice as much as you want and during you will be told how many blue dots you guessed correctly!

NOTE: Practice at least 4 times so you can get an idea of the various speed/density combinations you will encounter with each technique/trail combination!
